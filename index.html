I am McKenzie Larson (she/her), and I study Colorado weather.

I investigate the characteristics of Central U.S. extratropical cyclones and use machine learning to better understand and forecast them.

I also use weather and climate models to explore how Colorado Front Range downslope windstorms may change in a future climate.
